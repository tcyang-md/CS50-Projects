About


Who am I?

My name is Chris, I'm from Potomac, Maryland and I love to ski. I first learned to ski when my friend's father taught me almost 10 years ago and I've been skiing ever since! I try to Challenge myself everytime I do ski to keep improving every season.


Favorite Ski Resorts

Since I live on the east coast, it's very costly for me to travel with my equipment around the country so I've primarily skied driving distance from my house. Seven Springs, Whitetail, and Liberty are some of my favorites close to the DC metro area. The only resorts that I've been to besides the ones near my are Blue Mountain and Mount St. Louis Moonstone in Ontario, Canada were both extremely fun to ski this past winter season.



What am I currently working on?

This past season, I've been trying to deepen my edge angle, maintain a more balanced skiing stance, separate my lower body from my upper body, as well as maintaining control and rhythm throughout blacks and double blacks. It's extremely satisfying to progress in skiing because I'm able to feel my momentum shift much more smoothly as I ski. I'm hoping to learn how to ride the terrain park more confidently and ski in the Northern Applachian Mountains, American Rockies, Canadian Rockies, as well as Europe some day.
